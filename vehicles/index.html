Components of all rockets in detail

Crew Capsule

A pressurized environmentally friendly capsule for 6 people with big windows.

Ring and grid fins

Aerodynamically designed to precisely land on Earth without landing in a crash.

Drag wings

These are deployed to reduce speed and improve perpendicular flight of rocket to the ground.

Biofuel

All rockets include biofuel engines rather than traditional carbon engine in an effort to reduce any negative impacts on the environment. In addition, biofuels sustain longer durations than old rockets and reach faster speeds of 30, 000 mph to escape Earth's orbit and enter the International Karman Line.
