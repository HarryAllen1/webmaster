Biofuel

All rockets include biofuel engines rather than traditional engines in an effort to reduce negative environmental impacts from the rocket. In addition, biofuels sustain longer durations than old rockets and reach faster speeds of 30, 000 mph to escape Earth's orbit and enter the International Karman Line.
