The Andante rocket is a semi-lightweight launch vehicle that provides the foundation for space tourism. It can send a maximum of 6 astronauts to the edge of space. Its lightweight payload, reusable rocket boosters and unparalleled power made by our engineers is built for the comfort of human space travel. The Tempo is a more refined version of the Andante but with more engines and boosters to sustain one full orbit around the Earth. The Tempo can hold 10 people in total.

Components of all rockets in detail

Crew Capsule

A pressurized environmentally friendly capsule for 6 people with big windows.

Ring and grid fins

Aerodynamically designed to precisely land on Earth without landing in a crash.

Drag wings

These are deployed to reduce speed and improve perpendicular flight of rocket to the ground.

Biofuel Engines

The ABE-2 (Astro Bio Engine 2) in the Astro launch system will launch the rocket with biofuel to prevent any severe impacts on the environment. The ABE-2 is automatically capable of reaching immense speeds of 7000 mph and slowing to 3 mph.
